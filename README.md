# Parch and Posey Sales Analysis

Power BI analysis of sales, orders, and account performance for Parch and Posey, a paper company.

![Dashboard Screenshot](screenshots/dashboard.png)

## Project Overview
This project explores Parch and Posey’s sales data to understand:
- Which accounts and regions drive the most revenue
- How order volume and sales vary by paper type and channel
- Opportunities to improve profitability and sales strategy

## Key Insights
- **Top Performing Regions/Accounts**: Identify where the bulk of revenue comes from
- **Product Mix**: Compare sales across paper types - gloss, poster, standard
- **Sales Channel**: Analyze performance of direct vs reseller channels
- **Order Trends**: Spot seasonality and high/low periods in orders
